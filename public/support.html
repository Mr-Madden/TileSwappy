🎮 How to Play

Game Basics

TileSwappy is a daily puzzle game where you rotate and swap gradient tiles to match adjacent edges:

  • Click two separate tiles to swap their positions
  • Click and drag tiles to the left or right to rotate it 90 degrees clockwise
  • Match all edges - when adjacent tiles have matching gradient colors, you've solved the puzzle!
  • New puzzles daily - a fresh puzzle is available each day at midnight
💡 Pro Tip: Start by matching corner pieces, then work your way inward. Look for unique color combinations to help orient tiles correctly!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to create an account?
A: No! TileSwappy works without any login or registration. Just tap "Touch to Start" and play immediately. Your progress is saved locally on your device.
Q: How does the streak system work?
A: Complete a puzzle each day to maintain your streak. Your current streak and longest streak are tracked automatically. Miss a day and your streak resets, but you can always start a new one!
Q: Can I play previous puzzles?
A: Yes! Use the calendar icon to browse and play puzzles from the past 7 days. Completing past puzzles counts toward your statistics but won't restore a broken streak.
Q: What are the stars on the calendar?
A: Gold stars (⭐) indicate puzzles you've completed. The calendar shows your progress and current streak status for the past week.
Q: Why did I lose my progress?
A: Your game data is stored in your browser's local storage. Progress can be lost if you:
  • Clear your browser data/cache
  • Use incognito/private browsing mode
  • Switch browsers or devices
  • Uninstall and reinstall the app
Unfortunately, because we don't use accounts, we cannot recover lost progress. To prevent this, avoid clearing browser data for TileSwappy.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Game Won't Load

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page (F5 or Cmd+R)
  • Clear your browser cache and reload
  • Try a different browser
  • Disable browser extensions that might interfere (ad blockers, etc.)

Tiles Won't Move or Rotate

  • Make sure you're tapping directly on the tile (not between tiles)
  • For swapping, press and hold, then drag to the target tile
  • Try refreshing the page if controls become unresponsive
  • On mobile, ensure you're not accidentally triggering page scroll
