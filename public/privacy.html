Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 19, 2025

1. Overview

TileSwappy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data in our puzzle game application. We believe in transparency and your right to know exactly what information we do and don't collect.

The Bottom Line: TileSwappy does not require accounts or login credentials, and therefore does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

TileSwappy does not require accounts or login, and therefore we do not collect:

  • Personal information - No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or postal addresses
  • Account credentials - No usernames, passwords, or authentication data
  • Location data - No GPS coordinates or location tracking
  • Contact information - No access to your contacts or address book
  • Device identifiers - No unique device IDs or advertising identifiers
  • Biometric data - No fingerprints, face scans, or other biometric information
  • Payment information - No credit cards or financial data (the game is completely free)

3. Local Data Storage

Your game data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage technology. This includes:

  • Puzzle completion status - Which puzzles you've solved
  • Solve times - How long it took you to complete each puzzle
  • Current streak - Your consecutive days of puzzle solving
  • Longest streak - Your best streak achievement
  • Game statistics - Total puzzles completed, average times, etc.
  • Preference settings - Any game settings you've configured
Important: This data remains on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers. Clearing your browser data or switching devices will erase your progress, as there is no cloud backup or account system.

4. Third-Party Services

While TileSwappy itself doesn't collect personal data, we use third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdSense:

  • TileSwappy displays advertisements through Google AdSense to support the free service
  • Google may collect anonymous usage data and use cookies for ad personalization
  • Google's data collection is governed by their privacy policy, not ours
  • You can learn more at: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising at: https://www.google.com/settings/ads

5. Analytics

We may use basic web analytics to understand general usage patterns such as:

  • Total number of page views
  • Traffic sources (where visitors come from)
  • General geographic region (country/state level only)
  • Browser and device types

This data is anonymous and aggregated - it does not identify individual users and cannot be linked back to you.

6. Cookies

TileSwappy uses minimal cookies and browser storage:

  • localStorage - To save your game progress (essential for the game to function)
  • Third-party cookies - Google AdSense may set cookies for advertising purposes

You can control cookie settings in your browser, but disabling localStorage will prevent the game from saving your progress.

7. Children's Privacy

TileSwappy does not knowingly collect any personal information from users of any age, as we do not collect personal information at all. The game is suitable for all ages and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by not collecting any personal data.

Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is protected while playing TileSwappy, as there is no way for personal information to be collected or shared.

8. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no centralized database of user information that could be compromised in a data breach. Your local game data is only as secure as your device and browser.

We recommend:

  • Using a secure device with up-to-date software
  • Being cautious about clearing browser data if you want to preserve progress
  • Using trusted browsers and keeping them updated

9. Your Rights and Choices

Because we don't collect personal data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) don't apply. However, you have complete control:

  • Local data - You can clear your game progress at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Cookies - You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings
  • Ads - You can opt out of personalized ads through Google's ad settings

10. International Users

TileSwappy is accessible worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect personal information, there are no international data transfer concerns.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Material changes will be communicated through the app or website.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

12. Legal Compliance

TileSwappy complies with applicable privacy laws including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - EU
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - California, USA
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) - USA

Our no-collection approach means we're inherently compliant with most privacy regulations.
