Gameplay Basics

How do I play TileSwappy?
TileSwappy is a puzzle game where you arrange 9 tiles to create complete images—gradients, photos, or artwork. Click or tap a tile to select it, then click another tile to swap their positions. Swipe or drag any tile to turn it clockwise or counterclockwise. The goal is to arrange all tiles so adjacent edges match perfectly, revealing the complete image across the entire 3x3 grid. For detailed strategies, check out our comprehensive How to Play guide.
Is there a time limit?
No! You can take as much time as you need to solve each puzzle. While your completion time is tracked and affects your score, there's no strict deadline. The puzzles remain available forever from its release, giving you the freedom to solve it whenever convenient. We believe puzzle games should be relaxing, not stressful.
How difficult are the puzzles?
TileSwappy puzzles are designed to be challenging but solvable within 5-10 minutes for most players. Beginners might take 15-20 minutes initially, but solve times naturally decrease with practice. Puzzles feature both smooth color gradients and vibrant photographs/artwork, each with their own unique challenges. All puzzles have a single correct solution and are tested for solvability before release. The difficulty varies day-to-day, ensuring fair competition on leaderboards. Every puzzle receives the same careful design attention.
Can I play previous days' puzzles?
Yes! All puzzles remain accessible in the archive for easy gradient puzzles or the daily calendar that has customized puzzle pictures. This feature is perfect for practice, exploring different puzzle types, or playing at your own pace. You can play any puzzle at any time—there's no time limit or daily restriction. Your statistics track overall progress regardless of which puzzle you solve or when you solve it.
Can I play puzzles in any order?
Absolutely! All puzzles are always available. There's no requirement to play them in order or on specific days. Your overall statistics (total puzzles solved, best scores, average times) track your progress. Play at your own pace—whether that's one puzzle a day, several in a row, or whenever you have time. No pressure, no deadlines!
Can I replay today's puzzle?
Yes! You can always replay any puzzle as many times as you'd like. It will only help improve your overall score. Additional attempts are great for practice, trying different strategies, or improving your personal time. Note the reset button in the settings menu is to start completely fresh.
Do all puzzles use the same style?
No! We feature various puzzle types to keep challenges interesting: smooth gradient transitions, vibrant photographs, artistic imagery, and creative visual designs. Some puzzles flow with color (warm to cool), others feature recognizable scenes or subjects, and some combine multiple visual elements. This variety ensures each day brings a unique challenge. Over time, you'll develop pattern recognition skills that help you quickly identify puzzle types and adapt your solving strategy.

Scoring & Statistics

How is my score calculated?
Your score is based on multiple factors:
  • Completion: Base points for solving the puzzle
  • Move efficiency: Fewer swaps and rotations increase your score
  • Time bonus: Faster completion times award bonus points
  • Perfect game bonus: Solving without any incorrect placements provides a significant multiplier
The scoring algorithm rewards both speed and accuracy, so rushing through with many mistakes won't beat a methodical, efficient solve.
What counts as a "move"?
Each action that changes the puzzle state counts as a move:
  • Swapping two tiles = 1 move
  • Rotating a tile = 1 move
  • Selecting/deselecting tiles = 0 moves (doesn't count)
The most efficient solves minimize total moves by planning ahead. Expert players often complete puzzles in 30-40 moves, while beginners might use 60-80 moves. Practice improves efficiency!
Is there any time pressure to play?
None at all! TileSwappy is designed for flexible, pressure-free play. All puzzles remain available indefinitely. Play them in any order, at any time. There are no daily requirements, no deadlines, and no penalties for taking breaks. Whether you play daily or once a week, your progress is saved and all puzzles are waiting for you.
Where can I see my statistics?
Click the statistics icon (usually a bar chart or trophy icon) to view your comprehensive stats:
  • Total puzzles solved
  • Average completion time
  • Average completion time
  • Average moves per puzzle
  • Best score and fastest time
  • Success rate (percentage of attempted puzzles completed)
  • Daily performance graph
Stats update in real-time as you play and persist across devices when logged in.

Technical Questions

What devices and browsers does TileSwappy support?
TileSwappy is built as a responsive web application that works on:
  • Desktop browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
  • Mobile browsers: iOS Safari, Chrome on Android (latest versions)
  • Tablets: iPad, Android tablets
We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience. The game automatically adapts its interface to your screen size, providing optimal layouts for phones, tablets, and desktops. Native iOS and Android apps are currently in development.
How do I install TileSwappy as an app?
On iOS (iPhone/iPad):
  1. Open TileSwappy in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
  3. Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Name it "TileSwappy" and tap "Add"
On Android:
  1. Open TileSwappy in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu
  3. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. Confirm installation
On Desktop:
  1. Visit TileSwappy in Chrome or Edge
  2. Look for the install icon in the address bar
  3. Click "Install" when prompted
Once installed, TileSwappy will open in its own window like a native app!
My puzzle isn't loading. What should I do?
Try these troubleshooting steps in order:
  1. If in a web browser, refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  2. Clear your browser cache and reload
  3. Check your internet connection
  4. Try a different browser
  5. Disable browser extensions temporarily (ad blockers sometimes interfere)
  6. Check if your browser is up to date
If problems persist after trying these steps, please contact us through our support page with details about your device, browser, and the issue you're experiencing.
    If on a mobile device, try closing and reopening the app or browser. Restarting your device can also resolve temporary glitches.
Does TileSwappy drain my battery?
TileSwappy is optimized for minimal battery usage. We use efficient rendering techniques and avoid unnecessary animations when they're not visible. A typical 5-minute puzzle session should use less than 1% battery on most modern smartphones. However, keeping your screen brightness very high or playing for extended periods will naturally consume more power. We continuously optimize performance to ensure TileSwappy remains a lightweight, battery-friendly experience.

Troubleshooting

Why are colors appearing wrong?
Color display issues can stem from several sources:
  • Monitor calibration: Your display might need color calibration
  • Browser color management: Some browsers modify colors; try a different browser
  • Night shift/blue light filters: These can distort puzzle colors; disable temporarily
  • Accessibility settings: High contrast or color inversion settings may affect display
  • Graphics drivers: Outdated drivers can cause rendering issues; update them
TileSwappy uses standard web colors (sRGB), so if colors look correct on other websites, they should display properly here. If problems persist, try viewing on a different device to determine if it's device-specific.
The game feels laggy or slow. How can I fix this?
Performance issues are usually solvable:
  1. Close other browser tabs (each tab uses memory)
  2. Close resource-intensive applications
  3. Try a different browser (Chrome is usually fastest)
  4. Disable animations in Settings → Reduce animations
  5. Clear browser cache and cookies
  6. Update your browser to the latest version
  7. Restart your device
TileSwappy is lightweight and should run smoothly on devices from the last 5-7 years. If you're on a very old device, consider the "Performance mode" option in Settings which disables non-essential features.
My progress disappeared after clearing browser data!
This can happen for a few reasons:
  • Browser data cleared: Clearing cookies/cache removes local data if not logged in
  • Different device: Progress doesn't sync across devices without an account
  • Incognito/private mode: Progress isn't saved in private browsing
To prevent data loss, create an account so progress syncs to our servers. This protects against local data loss and ensures your statistics persist across devices.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
We appreciate bug reports! Please submit them through our support page with as much detail as possible:
  • What happened (the bug)
  • What you expected to happen
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Device and browser information
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)
  • Date and time the bug occurred
We review all bug reports and prioritize fixes based on severity and frequency. Critical bugs affecting gameplay get immediate attention. You'll typically receive a response within 24-48 hours.
