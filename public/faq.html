Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TileSwappy

🎮 Gameplay Questions

Q: How do I play TileSwappy?
TileSwappy is a visual puzzle game where you arrange 9 tiles to recreate a complete image. Click two tiles to swap their positions, and click and drag tiles left or right to rotate them. The goal is to match all adjacent edges so the image flows seamlessly. Check out our How to Play guide for detailed instructions.
Q: What types of puzzles are available?
TileSwappy features multiple puzzle types including smooth color gradients, photographs, and artistic images. Each puzzle type challenges your pattern recognition and visual perception in different ways, keeping the game fresh and engaging.
Q: Is there a time limit?
No! Take as much time as you need to solve each puzzle. While your completion time is tracked for scoring purposes, there's no pressure or countdown timer forcing you to rush. Play at your own pace and enjoy the puzzle-solving experience.
Q: Can I play previous puzzles?
Yes! All puzzles remain accessible in our archive. Unlike daily puzzle games that lock you into a schedule, TileSwappy lets you play any puzzle at any time. Browse past puzzles, replay favorites, or catch up on ones you missed.
Q: How do I know if tiles are correctly placed?
When adjacent tiles are correctly positioned and rotated, their edges will match perfectly creating a seamless visual transition. You'll see the gradient or image flow naturally from one tile to the next. When all 9 tiles are correct, the complete image will be revealed.

💾 Account & Progress

Q: Do I need to create an account?
No account required! TileSwappy works immediately without any registration or login. Your progress and statistics are saved locally on your device, so you can start playing right away.
Q: Why did I lose my progress?
Your game data is stored in your browser's local storage. Progress can be lost if you clear your browser data/cache, use incognito/private browsing mode, switch browsers or devices, or uninstall and reinstall the app. To prevent this, avoid clearing browser data for TileSwappy.
Q: Can I sync progress across devices?
Currently, each device maintains its own separate progress since data is stored locally. There's no cloud sync feature at this time. We're exploring account-based syncing for future updates.
Q: How are statistics calculated?
Your stats track total puzzles completed, average solve times, best times, and other performance metrics. All calculations are done locally on your device and are never shared or uploaded to our servers.

🎯 Features & Settings

Q: Are there different difficulty levels?
Puzzles vary naturally in difficulty based on the image complexity and gradient patterns. Some puzzles with distinct features are easier to solve, while subtle gradients provide more challenge. All puzzles use the same 3x3 grid format.
Q: Can I get hints?
Currently, TileSwappy doesn't include a hint system. The challenge is part of the experience! If you're stuck, try our strategy guide which offers techniques like the corner-first method and pattern recognition tips.
Q: Does TileSwappy work offline?
Once a puzzle loads, you can complete it offline. However, you'll need an internet connection to load new puzzles and to display advertisements that support the free service.
Q: Is TileSwappy really free?
Yes! TileSwappy is completely free to play with all features unlocked. We show tasteful advertisements to support development and keep the game free for everyone. There are no premium tiers, paywalls, or in-app purchases.

🔧 Technical Questions

Q: What browsers are supported?
TileSwappy works best on modern browsers: Chrome 90+, Edge 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+ on desktop, and iOS Safari 14+, Chrome Mobile 90+ on mobile devices. Make sure JavaScript and LocalStorage are enabled.
Q: Why isn't the game loading?
Try these troubleshooting steps: check your internet connection, refresh the page (F5 or Cmd+R), clear your browser cache, try a different browser, or disable browser extensions that might interfere. If issues persist, visit our support page.
Q: The tiles won't move or rotate. What's wrong?
Make sure you're clicking directly on tiles (not between them). For swapping, click one tile then click another. For rotating, click and drag left or right on the tile. If controls become unresponsive, try refreshing the page.
Q: Can I play on mobile?
Absolutely! TileSwappy is fully responsive and works great on phones and tablets. Use tap to select/swap tiles and swipe left/right to rotate them. The interface adapts to your screen size for the best experience.

🔒 Privacy & Data

Q: What data does TileSwappy collect?
TileSwappy doesn't collect personal information since there are no accounts or logins. Your game data is stored locally on your device only. We may use anonymous analytics to understand general usage patterns. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.
Q: Are my puzzle solutions private?
Yes! All your gameplay data, including solutions, times, and statistics, stays on your device. We never see or store your individual puzzle solutions on our servers.
Q: Why do I see advertisements?
Advertisements through Google AdSense help support TileSwappy and keep it free for everyone. Ads are served by third-party providers who may use cookies for ad personalization. You can learn more in our Privacy Policy.

💡 Tips & Strategies

Q: How can I improve my solve times?
Practice is key! Start by mastering the corner-first method, learn to recognize gradient patterns quickly, and develop efficient rotation habits. Our 5 Strategies guide offers detailed techniques used by top players.
Q: What's the best strategy for beginners?
Start with corner pieces—they're easiest to identify. Look for tiles with the most extreme colors (lightest/darkest). Once corners are placed, work on edge pieces, then fill in the center. Check out our complete strategy guide.
Q: Are there leaderboards?
Currently, TileSwappy tracks personal statistics only. Since there are no user accounts, we don't have global leaderboards yet. This feature is being considered for future updates with account-based functionality.
