About TileSwappy

A puzzle game that celebrates visual beauty through patterns, gradients, and photography

The Vision

TileSwappy was born from a simple idea: what if there was a puzzle game that combined the meditative quality of arranging patterns with the accessible format of casual puzzle games? The result is a unique puzzle experience that challenges your visual perception and pattern recognition skills while providing a moment of calm whenever you play—whether you're reconstructing a vibrant gradient, a stunning photograph, or artistic imagery.

Unlike word games or number puzzles, TileSwappy taps into a different part of your brain; the visual cortex that processes colors, patterns, and spatial relationships. Each puzzle is a small work of art that you reconstruct through careful observation and logical thinking.

Our Mission

To create a puzzle experience that's accessible, beautiful, and genuinely satisfying to solve. Every puzzle should feel like both a challenge and a reward, with that perfect "aha!" moment when the image finally comes together.

What Makes TileSwappy Different

🎨 Visual First

Instead of words or numbers, you work with images, colors, and patterns, engaging a different kind of problem-solving.

⚡ Always Available

All puzzles accessible anytime. Play at your own pace without daily pressure or time limits.

🧠 Accessible Depth

Easy to understand but challenging to master. No special knowledge required—just observation and logic.

The Development Journey

TileSwappy was developed by Mad_Den Gaming Co., a solo development studio focused on creating thoughtful digital experiences. The game is built with modern web technologies—React and TypeScript on the frontend, with Supabase powering the backend infrastructure.

The development process involved numerous iterations on the puzzle generation algorithm. Early versions used random color selection for gradients, but these puzzles often felt arbitrary. The breakthrough came when we expanded to include not just mathematical gradients, but also beautiful photographs and artwork. This diversity ensures that every puzzle has an inherent logic and visual appeal, whether it's a smooth color transition or a stunning image waiting to be reconstructed.

"I wanted to create something that felt as good to solve as it looked. TileSwappy is my attempt to blend the joy of puzzle-solving with the appreciation of visual aesthetics."
— Mr._Madden, Creator

The Technology

Behind TileSwappy's simple interface lies sophisticated technology designed to ensure a smooth, reliable experience:

  • React & TypeScript: Modern frontend framework ensuring type-safe, maintainable code
  • Supabase Backend: Real-time database handling puzzle delivery, stats tracking, and leaderboards
  • Cloudflare Pages: Lightning-fast global deployment with edge caching
  • Progressive Web App: Install TileSwappy on any device for an app-like experience
  • Responsive Design: Seamless experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

Design Philosophy

Every aspect of TileSwappy's design serves the core puzzle experience:

Minimalist Interface: No distractions, no clutter. The puzzle takes center stage, with controls that fade into the background until needed. This isn't about flashy graphics—it's about creating space for focused problem-solving.

Smooth Interactions: Every tap, swipe, and rotation feels responsive and natural. Animations provide feedback without slowing you down. The interface disappears, leaving just you and the puzzle.

Celebration of Visual Beauty: Each puzzle showcases visual artistry—whether it's the mathematical elegance of color gradients or the captured moment of a photograph. We carefully curate puzzles that are both challenging to solve and visually appealing once complete. Solving a TileSwappy puzzle should feel like revealing a hidden work of art.

Accessibility Matters: We've worked to ensure TileSwappy is playable by as many people as possible, with considerations for different visual abilities and input methods. Puzzles are designed to be solvable through pattern recognition, not just color perception.

The Community

TileSwappy players come from all walks of life—students solving puzzles between classes, professionals taking brain breaks at work, retirees making it part of their morning routine, and puzzle enthusiasts chasing leaderboard positions.

What unites the community is an appreciation for the meditative quality of the game. In our fast-paced, attention-fragmented world, TileSwappy offers a few minutes of focused, satisfying problem-solving.
